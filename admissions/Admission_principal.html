Principal's Corner

Dr B P Patil

B.E., M.E., Ph.D(Electronics Tech), DBM, MMS, FIETE, FIEI, LMISTE, LMISoI, Sr Member IEEE
Principal at AIT

Received his Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics Engineering from Amravati University, India in 1990 and Masters in Engineering degree from MNREC (NIT) Allahabad, India in 1996. He received Ph.D. in Electronics Technology from Guru Nanak Dev Univ., Amritsar, India in year 2000. Along with this he also did DBM and MMS from North Maharashtra University in 1993 and 1994 respectively.

He is having enriched teaching experience of 27 years and 5 years in industry. He has published 174 research papers in various international and national referred journals and conferences. He has filed 7 patents, five copy rights and published two books. He is recognized Ph.D. guide from University of Pune. Under his able guidance 10 students completed Ph.D. in engineering field. His area of Interest includes Wireless Communication, Wireless Sensor Network, Nonconventional energy and Signal processing.

He has participated in many Seminars and Conferences and delivered keynote address and chaired sessions. He is reviewed research manuscripts for refereed international journals.

He is holding the post of Principal at AIT since Feb 2015. Under his tenure AIT is reaccredited 3rd time NBA and NAAC with ‘A’ Grade, received best professional college award from Univ of Pune in 2017. He was instrumental in starting PG course in Mechanical Engineering and increase in intake of UG Computer and E&TC.

He is Sr Member, IEEE, fellow of Institution of Engineers, IETE and life member of ISTE and Instrument Society of India.

He is recipient of “Sir Thomas Ward Memorial Medal” from the Institution of Engineering, “Education Leadership Award” by Dewang Mehta National Education Award and 50 most influential Principals of India(Education).

Received CMI (Chartered Management Institute) Level 5 Certificate in Management and Leadership from UK.
