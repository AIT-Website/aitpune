Overview

The National Education Policy (NEP) is a comprehensive framework aimed at transforming the education system in India. Introduced in 2020, it emphasizes holistic development, flexibility, and the integration of technology in education. The NEP focuses on foundational literacy and numeracy, vocational education, and multidisciplinary learning. It proposes significant reforms such as the introduction of a new curriculum structure, the establishment of a National Educational Technology Forum, and the restructuring of regulatory bodies. The policy aims to make education more inclusive and equitable, catering to the diverse needs of learners, while also fostering critical thinking, creativity, and innovation.
