BMW Twinpower Turbo In-Line 4-Cylinder 2.0 Litre Diesel Engine


  • Combination of common rail direct injection and a turbocharger with variable turbine geometry
  • Lightweight aluminium construction
  • Output of 140 KW/190HP and a maximum torque of 400NM at 1,750-2,500 RPM
  • Maximum performance, excellent response and power delivery at all speeds for high efficiency


BMW Eight Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission


  • Highly smooth gear shifts for optimal power development
  • Gears reduce the number of revolutions at high speed, thereby reducing the fuel consumption and engine noise, while increasing sportiness
  • Central component in BMW efficient dynamics
