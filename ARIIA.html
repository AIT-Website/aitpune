Overview

ARIIA, the Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements, serves as a beacon of recognition for our college's commitment to fostering innovation. It highlights our relentless pursuit of groundbreaking ideas and solutions that address real-world challenges. Through ARIIA, we showcase our dedication to nurturing an environment where creativity flourishes, driving positive change in our society. Participation in ARIIA underscores our role as a catalyst for innovation, inspiring our students and faculty to push the boundaries of knowledge and drive transformative impact.
