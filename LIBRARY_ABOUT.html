About Library

AIT has a well-stocked library having collection of books, national and international print journals and e-Resources. The state-of-art air conditioned reading hall provided with wi-fi facility has a seating capacity of 156. The Library maintains a separate reference collection consisting of encyclopedias, dictionaries, handbooks, data books, etc. It has a digital section having audio/video resources, CDs, CBT and NPTEL videos. This section also provides access to e- journals. Library is fully automated. OPAC search and reissue transactions are made available on Intranet and Internet.
