Overview

The National Board of Accreditation (NBA) is an autonomous body established by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) in India. Its primary function is to assess and accredit technical education programs offered by institutions in the country. NBA evaluates various parameters such as curriculum, faculty quality, infrastructure, student outcomes, and institutional management to ensure quality assurance and improvement in technical education.
